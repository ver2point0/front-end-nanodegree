Rabbit

Male rabbits are called bucks. Females rabbits are does. Young rabbits are kittens or kits.

  • Lifespan: 3 years
  • Mass: 0.88 - 4.4 lbs
  • Daily sleep: 8.4 hours
  • Length: 7.9 - 20 in.

Rabbits are often used as a symbol of fertility or rebirth, and have long been associated with Spring and Easter as the Easter Bunny. They appear in folklore and modern children's stories, often but not invariably as sympathetic characters.
